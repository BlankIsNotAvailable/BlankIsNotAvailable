  • 500g Flouer
  • 500g Sugar
  • 250g Melted Butter
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 11⁄2 tsp. Baking Poweder
  1. Preheat the oven to 250Â°C
  2. Add the Flouer and Baking Powder into a bowl and wisk to combine them.
  3. Melt the butter and slowly add it to the sugar mixing constantly.
  4. Combine the Flouer and Baking Powder mix with the sugar and butter mix, stir well until homogeneous. Add both eggs and mix well.
  5. Pour into a pre-oiled baking dish and place it into the pre-heated oven for 30min.
  6. Let cool on a wired rack and optionally dust with powdered sugar.
